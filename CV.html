About Qiu

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Graduate Institute of Biomedical Electronics and Bioinformatics (BEBI), National Taiwan University. My research interests include queueing theory, computer network, machine learning and optimal decision making. To be honest, my interests are quite strange in BEBI. In the same college, I always belong to Dept. of CS, which is my previous base.

I have no idea that I have majored in computer science for many years. I sucessfully did worse in all subjects and the mathematics is one of the hopeless things I messed up when I was a high school student. In fact, I messed up many things in high school. So it stands to reason that I will meet the mathematics again. It feels like the predetermined binding force between STEM and I. Good or bad, it can't bring love, health, but money, hope so.

I am simple, not ambitious. I'm not particularly eager to follow the historical experiences from the outside world. It seems like the \(\epsilon-greedy\) strategy in reinfoecement learning. I'd like to set the \(\epsilon\) (I call it the "romanticism" factor) to be 0.9 when a youth, to explore my life with eagerness by neglecting the external experiences with probability 0.9. I believe that following the "brave equation," the total reward I get when I leave the world will not be too bad, even though there have been some hopeless moments in my passed life-time.
